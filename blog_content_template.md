## âœï¸ Writing Style Guidelines

### Tone:
- **Professional but approachable** - Not stuffy or academic
- **Confident but humble** - Share learnings, acknowledge limitations
- **Clear and direct** - No jargon unless explained
- **Actionable** - Always provide practical value

### Voice:
- First person (I, my, we)
- Use personal anecdotes
- Share real experiences
- Be authentic

### Formatting:
- **Short paragraphs** (3-4 lines max)
- **Bullet points** for lists
- **Bold** for emphasis (sparingly)
- **Italics** for quotes or emphasis
- **Headings** for clear structure
- **Whitespace** for readability

### Engagement:
- Ask questions
- Use "you" to address reader
- Include calls-to-action
- Encourage comments
- Be conversational
